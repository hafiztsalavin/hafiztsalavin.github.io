SMONIC!

Design of a Smart Monitoring and Control System for Hydroponic Plants Based on Android Smartphone is a prototype that refers to an integrated system between smart hydroponic and smartphone.

The smart hydroponic have sensors in a microcontroller to sends data in real time about plant conditions. The sensors that is connected to the internet will transfer the information of the condition plant to a smartphone. Then prototype equipped with a webcam to take pictures of plants and then processed, the goal is to visualize the condition of the plant in real time. Furthermore, in this prototype plant conditions can be controlled automatically and manually through an Android application for the plant growth optimal.

From this idea, I and my team got a silver medal for category Technology, IoT, and Apps on JDIE 2019 in Tokyo, Japan.

Sign Language Recognition Using CNN !

Sign Language Recognition Using CNN was developed as my thesis defense. The objective of my thesis is to predict an alphabet sign language. Computer vision and deep learning technology used to develop the project.

I used CNN as one of the deep learning methods for classification in my study case. This project was developed with Keras as a neural network framework and TensorFlow as the backend of deep learning.

The main objective of this project is to make an interface between normal people and that deaf and dumb people with computer vision. The hand images were taken using a camera and processed to be recognized. For recognizing the image, a classification model is built to identify the sign alphabet using CNN with sequential model, and for the real time application, the hand gesture sign is detected using OpenCV and fed to the model to make the final prediction. The prediction also can be saved and set to be words
