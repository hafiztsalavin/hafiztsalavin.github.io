Intermediate Level Generasi Gigih

Hello everyone !

Since May 11 2021, I have been accepted as a participants at Generasi Gigih – Back End Engineeer Path from Yayasan Anak Bangsa Bisa. And today I was receive email from generasi gigih that I moving to the next round of generasi gigih program which is the intermediate level. I was very happy because in the intermediate level we will learn more deeper about concepts and fundamentals about backend engineering from engineer gojek, directly. Amazing. The intermediate phase starts on Monday 5 July 2021.

So first of all, I’ll explain what is Generasi Gigih by Yayasan Anak Bangsa Bisa. Yayasan Anak Bangsa Bisa is a non-profit organization founded by Gojek. YABB is committed to help society build back better. And Generasi Gigih is a bootcamp that was designed as a breakthrough for Indonesia’s young tech talent to catch up with the fast-paced tech industry with the right skill and mindset. I picked the Backend Engineer track and I learn fundamental of backend engineering with Ruby.

Previously, I passed the first stage as self learning with progate, cakap and XL future leader. I have learned basic Ruby, SQL etc with material related with backend engineering, and than with cakap I learn fundamental of software engineering communication with english obviously. With XL Future leader I learn tips and trick to growth mindset and personal branding, both cakap and XL Future leader as giving as a materials for soft skill. We also had the opportunity to join a webinar to hear stories and discuss from the great people at Gojek such as Aleisha Fiona, Opan Mustopah, and CEO of gojek Kevin Aluwi.

Well, maybe for next 2 month. This blog will be filling my journey and notes materials. Thank you!
